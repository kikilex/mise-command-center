The Apostle Paul, writing under the inspiration of the Holy Spirit, gave us a detailed description of the spiritual armor available to every believer. This is not merely symbolic language ‚Äî these are real spiritual weapons designed for real spiritual combat.

The Belt of Truth

The first piece of armor Paul mentions is the belt of truth. In Roman times, the belt held everything together and allowed the soldier to move freely. Truth operates the same way in our spiritual lives.

PRAYER

Heavenly Father, I put on the belt of truth today. Let Your truth guard my heart and mind against every lie of the enemy. Help me to walk in integrity and honesty in all my dealings. I reject every deception and embrace Your Word as the standard of truth in my life. In Jesus' name, Amen.

The Breastplate of Righteousness

The breastplate protected the soldier's vital organs, especially the heart. The righteousness of Christ protects our hearts from the accusations and condemnation of the enemy.

PRAYER

Lord, I put on the breastplate of righteousness. I stand in the righteousness of Christ, not my own. Guard my heart from condemnation and shame. Let me walk uprightly before You, protected by Your righteousness that covers me completely. In Jesus' name, Amen.
